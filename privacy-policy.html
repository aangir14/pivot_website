1. What Information Do We Collect?

  • Personal identification data (e.g., name, email address, phone number, birth date, gender, picture, photographs, mailing address)
  • Health-related data (e.g., blood reports data, weight, height, food preferences, nutrition data, workouts, steps, sleep data, SPO2, HRV, body temperature, medical conditions, health goals)
  • Payment information (only name and billing address, not card details)
  • Automatically collected data (e.g., IP address, device and browser information, usage patterns, location, metadata)
  • Data from third party devices, mobile and messaging apps (e.g., geo-location, device access, push notifications)
  • Usage data (e.g., cookies, pixel tags, analytics tools, mobile and web logs, timestamps, interaction data)
  • Information from third-party integrations (e.g., HealthKit, Google Fit, Apple Health, email, social media login data)
  • Data from Integrated Health Apps and devices: If you connect your Apple Watch or Apple Health, Morpheus Training, or similar applications with our Services, we may access certain health metrics (e.g., heart rate, HRV, step count, activity data, sleep). This data is only accessed with your explicit consent and is used solely to enhance our wellness recommendations and progress tracking.
  • Your Contributions: Information and content you provide, including content, communications and other information you provide when you use our Products and Services, including when you sign up for an account, create or share content, and message or communicate with others.

Some information – such as IP address and/or browser and device characteristics – is collected automatically when you visit our Website or access our Services or Apps.
