Your Leadership Team's Health Is Your Organisation's Foundation

Heart disease remains the leading cause of unexpected executive absence and long-term incapacity in India. Most cardiac events in leaders are preceded by years of silent, reversible risk.

Our Corporate HeartSpan programme identifies these early warning signs through advanced screening, personalised coaching, and evidence-based interventions — before they become crises.

5 Phases to Lasting Heart Health

Our comprehensive approach combines workshops, screening, personalised care, and ongoing support to create lasting organisational impact.

Phase 1 — Discovery

Understanding your organisation's goals...

A discovery call that helps us understand your organisation's goals, leadership profile and expectations. We tailor the program to fit your team's specific needs and challenges.

This initial consultation ensures the program aligns perfectly with your corporate culture and wellness objectives.

Phase 2 — Health Workshop

Science-backed session for leadership...

A science-backed session for senior leadership with Ankur Bhatia that creates clarity around cardiac risk and actionable next steps. Interactive, engaging, and immediately applicable.

Workshop Themes: The Stress-Heart Equation, Metabolic Health Beyond Fitness, Longevity Strategies, and Prevention Protocols.

Phase 3 — Comprehensive Health Screening

Complete health profile baseline...

Recommended blood tests and baseline measures to create a complete health profile for leaders. Professional, confidential, and comprehensive assessments.

Advanced diagnostics include cholesterol panels, inflammatory markers, metabolic health indicators, and cardiovascular risk factors.

Phase 4 — Personal Health Report & 1:1 Review

Personalized insights and consultation...

Every leader receives an easy-to-understand health report followed by a 30-minute 1:1 consultation to explain insights, risks and next steps. All data remains confidential and shared only with client permission.

The personal review ensures each executive understands their unique health profile and has a clear action plan.

Phase 5 — Program Enrollment & Ongoing Care

Continued support and monitoring...

Leaders may enrol in HeartSpan for personalised ongoing care, coaching and monitoring. Long-term support to ensure sustainable health improvements.

Continuous guidance from dedicated health coaches, nutritionists, and exercise physiologists ensures lasting results.
