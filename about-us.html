Our Story

The PIVOT Purpose

I started PIVOT because I believe health should be your greatest asset—not a barrier. For over 15 years, I’ve helped entrepreneurs, professionals, and families reclaim their health—not just to avoid illness, but to thrive in their careers, relationships, and purpose. As a certified Yoga Therapist, Nutrition Coach, and Behavior Change Specialist, I’ve seen firsthand how powerful it is when we blend modern science with timeless wisdom

But the real turning point came when my son faced a life-threatening illness. Conventional medicine reached its limits, and I was forced to look deeper. Through nutrition, yoga, and targeted lifestyle changes, I witnessed a transformation that changed my life—and my philosophy—forever. I realized that symptoms are not problems to fix; they’re messages. Clues to deeper imbalances we can decode and address.

That experience shaped the foundation of PIVOT. Here, we don’t chase trends or treat health like a checklist. We personalize. We guide. We simplify the overwhelming and bring clarity to the complex. Because when your health works for you, everything else—your performance, your relationships, your joy—falls into place. My mission is simple: to help you build a life where health fuels your potential, not limits it.

We help people take control of their health before chronic conditions take control of them.

Our mission is prevention with purpose—extending healthspan and improving quality of life through personalized, science-backed programs that fit real lives.

Whether you're a high-performing professional, a multitasking parent, or someone simply ready to stop surviving and start thriving, PIVOT gives you the tools, insights, and support to get there.

The Foundation: DOPH

Every PIVOT program is built on our core framework

D

Data-Driven

We use health data, lifestyle insights, and real-world context to create relevant, timely interventions that align with your body, your life, and your goals.

O

Outcome-Oriented

We go beyond generic metrics. Our programs aim for tangible improvements in how you feel, your symptoms, biomarkers, and overall function.

P

Personalized

Hyper-personalization considering your health status, behavioral patterns, lifestyle demands, and preferences—from nutrition to stress management.

H

Human Connection

While we leverage technology, lasting change happens through people. Our coaches build trust and walk the path with you—because healing is human.

Dr. Charu Arora

(PhD, MSc, RD)

Chief Nutrition Coach

PhD in Nutrition Registered Dietitian

Dr. Charu Arora is a highly accomplished Registered Dietitian with over 9 years of experience in clinical nutrition, specializing in obesity, diabetes, PCOD, hypertension, and fatty liver. She holds a PhD in Food and Nutrition from the University of Delhi and AIIMS, and is certified in Low FODMAP and Fertility Nutrition.

She has served in key academic and policy roles, including as a nutrition consultant at NITI Aayog and Assistant Professor at Lady Irwin College. She is the founder of Sankalp by Charu, where she leads personalized lifestyle interventions rooted in evidence-based nutrition.

Dr. Arora is a SCOPE Scholar awarded by the World Obesity Federation and has presented widely at national and international forums. Her work blends scientific rigor with practical coaching, making her a trusted expert in therapeutic and preventive nutrition.
